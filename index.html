Reflection Notebook

A quiet place to think in public or in private.

Reflection Notebook is a focused writing tool built on ProseMirror. It keeps the interface calm, stores your notes locally, and treats every page as a living document you can return to.

Open the app View the repository

Guiding principles

  • Local-first by default, no account wall.
  • Less chrome, more words.
  • Structure that grows with your thinking.
  • Export-friendly and portable.

How it works

The editor leans on ProseMirror for reliable document structure and keyboard-driven editing. Notes are stored in browser storage so you can work offline and keep your writing close.

Philosophy

Reflection Notebook is designed for deliberate thinking. It favors calm surfaces, gentle constraints, and patterns that encourage returning to a page rather than abandoning it.

Docs & roadmap

The documentation lives in the repository for now. PWA support and sync options are on the horizon once the core writing experience feels settled.
